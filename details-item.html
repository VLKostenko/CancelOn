Important Information

Cancelon does its best to protect its users. Cancelon mediates between Seller and Buyer to secure both sides. Once you completed your payment, we will take the following steps:


1. Cancelon will contact Seller to notify Seller about the transaction. Seller will have 24 hours to reply and accept your request. The reservation is non-refundable however, we will refund you in case Seller doesn’t accept the offer or if we don’t approve the reservation.

You can pay for the transaction both by a credit card and by PayPal.

The amount you will pay stays with Cancelon until a day after the check in day. This is to prevent Sellers from selling the reservation to more than one person.


2. Cancelon and Seller will contact the hotel to change the name on the reservation to your name. You will get a reservation confirmation email with your name.

Important Reminder – To make sure you receive our emails, please add support@cancelon.com to your contacts or safe sender list.
